RU Makers Laser Etched Pint Glasses Event

In the spirit of St. Patrick’s day, we’ll be having a laser etched pint glass fundraiser!We will be selling them for $8, and all the money goes back to the amazing projects we have at the space. There will also be a raffle where 10 people will win a free laser etched shot glass! To enter, you must buy a pint glass, like our RU Makers FB page and follow our instagram.

Elections

Get involved in the Rutgers and Makerspace communities by joining the RU Makers eboard. To run for a position sign up by filling out this Google form: goo.gl/Fb38kn Please read over the responsibilities of each position here: goo.gl/DzFmGu Please note that to run for President it is required that you have attended at least 50% of the meetings. You may run for multiple positions. Elections will be held on April 9th promptly at 7 PM. Here's the link to the Facebook event: https://www.facebook.com/events/381035962721884/ At elections everyone running for a position will be required to give a speech about why they are a good fit for the position and then we will hold a vote. Feel free to reach out the eboard if you have any questions or concerns at rumakerspaceclub@gmail.com or by messaging us.
